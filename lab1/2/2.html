Recipe #1

  • Time: 30 minute
  • Serves: _
Ingredients Quantity
boiled potatoes 4
carrot 1
sausage 300 gram
sausage 300 gram
boiled eggs 4
pickled cucumbers 4
canned green peas 1 can
mayonnaise 200-300 gram

Step 1:
Boil carrots and potatoes.

Step 2:
Hard boil the eggs.

Step 3:
Cut eggs, potatoes, sausage, carrots into small cubes of equal size.

Step 4:
Chop the cucumbers and squeeze a little so that there is not a lot of liquid in the salad.

Step 5:
Pour peas into the chopped ingredients (drain the water from it), chop the herbs, mix with mayonnaise. Season with salt and pepper. Refrigerate for 1 hour.
