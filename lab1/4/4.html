Information about mafia game:

Destiny Night - Role Distribution The choice is made using playing cards. Each card denotes a specific role in the game. Before starting the game, the necessary the number of cards equal to the number of players, and of them the deck is made for Game. Players are seated in any seats. The host shuffles the deck cards and asks all players to close their eyes - the "Night of Fate" is coming - all city ​​residents "fall asleep". Taking turns approaching each of the players, The presenter asks to open your eyes and at random draw a card from the deck, which determines the player's status in accordance with the suit chosen. Tranquility Day The host asks everyone to open their eyes and invites the players to start "Acquaintance" with each other - with the inhabitants of the city. Every player must look at your map in order to find out your playing role. Show other players are strictly prohibited from owning their card. No discussion is allowed on this day. Night Dating After the residents have met, the host announces “Night Dating ". All players close their eyes. The host says: "The mafia has woken up and is getting to know each other." After these words, the "mafiosi" open their eyes and find out who belongs clan "mafia". This should be done with extreme caution, since any movement may draw suspicion from nearby players. After that, the presenter announces: "The mafia met and fell asleep." The Mafia closes its eyes.

Day
The host announces: “The day has come. All residents of the city woke up. " All players open their eyes. Discussion begins. Players have the following information: who behaved how during the distribution, how and what they said the day before, movements in the night, changes in mood and behavior, etc. On based on all this, any conclusions can be drawn and assumptions. You can apply any arguments, both truthful and not. It is advisable to set aside some specific time for discussions, or moderator, at its discretion (for example, when the discussion goes to fight or, conversely, began to fade), stops the discussion and asks silence. After that, the host proposes to nominate suspects in involvement in the "mafia". Nominations are being nominated. Then the presenter gives each "candidate" the last word that can be decisive in the vote. Voting is in progress. The player who as a result of the vote found guilty, opens and shows everyone his card and leaves playing field. After the “killed” player has left the playing field, the leader announces: “Night has come. All residents of the city fell asleep. " All players close their eyes.
Night
At night, the presenter alternately wakes up the special characters of the game, and they secretly communicate their action. For example, it might look like this: Mafia move Host: "The mafia has woken up and is choosing its victim." "Mafia" opens his eyes and signs agree on which player should be "Kill". They point at him to the leader. After that, the presenter announces: "The Mafia made her choice and fell asleep." The Mafia closes its eyes. Sheriff's move The host announces: "The sheriff is out hunting." "Sheriff" opens his eyes and points to the presenter at the player whom he suspects of involvement in the "mafia". After that, the presenter announces: "The sheriff fired a shot and fell asleep." The Sheriff closes his eyes. Commissioner's move The presenter announces: "The commissioner is going to investigate." The commissioner opens his eyes and points to the leader at one of the players. The presenter shows thumbs up if the resident is honest, and down if "mafia". The host then announces: “The Commissioner has investigated and fell asleep. " The Commissioner closes his eyes. Doctor's turn The host announces: "The doctor comes out to help." The "Doctor" opens his eyes and points to the leader at any player. After that, the presenter announces: "The doctor provided help and fell asleep." The "doctor" closes his eyes.
Morning news
The host announces: “The day has come. All residents of the city woke up. " All players open their eyes. After that, the presenter covers all the events, that happened at night. It is advisable to deliver the speech in a stylistic registration for the game. 4 Then everything goes in a circle, but taking into account the fact that the players have for discussion new arguments appear. It is required to remember who nominated whom, who for whom he voted.
